# SHAWCO Digital Literacy Website 🌍

A single-page web application built for the **SHAWCO Digital Literacy Program** at the University of Cape Town, Cape Town, South Africa.

## About the Project

This website was developed during my internship at SHAWCO (UCT) as part of the **Tilting Futures / Global Citizen Year** program (Fall 2023). It was used across 8 weekly sessions with 20+ public school students from 2 different learner groups (primary school and adults) to help them document their journey through the digital literacy program.

Students used the platform to:
- Log their learnings and new skills (HTML, CSS, internet safety, CV writing)
- Describe their personal experience in the program
- Rate their sessions
- Write their dreams and career goals
- Leave messages for future students

The submissions were then used as the foundation for teaching professional resume/CV writing — students could see how to describe experiences and present themselves professionally.

## Technologies

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript (Vanilla)
- Google Fonts (Nunito)
- localStorage for data persistence

## Features

- ✅ Responsive single-page form with 5 sections
- ✅ Live progress bar tracking form completion
- ✅ Interactive skill checkboxes
- ✅ Star rating system
- ✅ Submission saves locally and displays a gallery of entries
- ✅ Colorful, intuitive UI designed for students with limited tech experience

## Impact

- Used by **20+ students** across public schools in Cape Town
- Delivered across **8 sessions/week** (2 sessions/day, Mon–Thu)
- Program expanded to **2 learner groups**: primary school students and adults
- Served as the foundation for teaching professional CV/resume building
